Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Same-day delivery within Delhi NCR for orders placed before 2 PM. Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days across India. International shipping varies by destination.

Do you provide insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer comprehensive insurance coverage for all shipments. Additional insurance can be purchased for high-value items.

What items cannot be shipped?

We cannot ship hazardous materials, illegal substances, perishable items, or items prohibited by law. Please contact us for specific restrictions.

How do I track my package?

Use the tracking ID provided at booking to track your package in real-time. You'll receive SMS and email updates at each stage.

What if my package is damaged?

Report any damage immediately upon delivery. We have a claims process and insurance coverage to handle such situations.

Can I change delivery address after booking?

Address changes are possible within 2 hours of booking. Contact our customer service team immediately for assistance.
